Welcome!

Need a little help learning about clocks and time? You've come to the right place! This page explains how the lessons work.

Understanding the Clock

  • Hour Hand: The shorter hand. It points to the hour.
  • Minute Hand: The longer hand. It points to the minutes.
  • Numbers: The numbers 1 through 12 show the hours.
  • Tick Marks: The small lines show the minutes (there are 60 minutes in an hour).

How to Use the Lessons

  • Navigation: Use the "Next Step" and "Previous Step" buttons (usually below the clock or activity) to move through the different parts of the lesson.

    How to Use the Lessons

    • Navigation: Use the "Next Step" and "Previous Step" buttons (usually below the clock or activity) to move through the different parts of the lesson.
    • Audio Instructions: Click the button to hear the instructions read aloud. You can also use and .
    • Interacting with the Clock: Some steps will ask you to click on parts of the clock (like the hands) or even drag the hands to set a time. Follow the audio and text instructions.
    • Answering Questions:
      • Multiple Choice: Click the button with the answer you think is correct.
      • Warm-Up Numbers: Click inside a light blue circle where a number is missing, then type the correct number using your keyboard.

    Troubleshooting

    • No Sound? Check if your computer's volume is turned up and not muted. Sometimes you need to click anywhere on the page first before audio can play automatically.
    • Something Not Working Right? The simplest fix is often to refresh or reload the web page (usually F5 key or a refresh button in your browser). Note that this might reset your progress on the current step.
    • Still Stuck? Don't worry! Ask a teacher or a grown-up for help.
